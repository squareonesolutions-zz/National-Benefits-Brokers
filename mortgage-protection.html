Mortgage Protection

Individuals and families purchase Mortgage Protection because they are looking to isolate their largest asset and liability. For most of us, our HOME is our single largest asset and liability.

Why Mortgage Protection Might Be Right for You:

  • Have you just purchased a new home?
  • Have you just refinanced an existing mortgage?
  • Have you decided to get married or have children?
  • Are you trying to isolate a specific asset or liability for a specific period of time?
  • Would you be unable to pay the mortgage if you became disabled or suffered from a critical illness that would keep you out of work for as little as 6-15 months or permanently

Life doesn't always fit into neat organized periods of time, with our protection programs you choose how long you want the coverage. For example, if there are 23 years remaining on your mortgage or if your youngest child will be graduating college in 14 years, it may not make sense for your family to purchase a 30-year term plan. Overall, insurance companies typically offer a basic solution for everyone in a 10, 15, 20 or 30 year plan. In reality, everyone has different needs for insurance coverage and having the option to select only the period of time you need, you avoid paying unnecessary costs associated with a longer duration.

Our Unique Mortgage Protection offers living benefits. Benefits you don't have to die to receive. It really is True Mortgage Protection. Think about it. Typically, we purchase life insurance and we believe that the death benefit can be used to pay of the house. This might be true, but what if we don't die? What if we are unable to work due to an injury or illness? The mortgage payment and other bills don't just go away. In fact, one of the major reasons families lose their home or are unable to make mortgage payments is not because of death, but due to injury or illness that prevents the bread winner from working, earning an income. Finally, there is a product that allows you to use the death benefits as you see fit. A product that allows you to accelerate your death benefit while living. A product that can truly protect your family and isolate your largest asset and liability, your home.
