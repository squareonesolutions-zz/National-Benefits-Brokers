Quality of Life Insurance

How Can Quality of Life...Insurance Help You?

Many people think that life insurance is only useful for a family after a death, and historically, that’s been the case. Traditional life insurance is designed to help families make ends meet when a family member dies prematurely...but what happens when someone suffers a major heart attack, invasive cancer or a stroke...and doesn’t die?

AGLA developed Quality of Life...Insurance to not only help families in the event of a premature death, but to also help if a primary breadwinner or caretaker suffers a major illness. That’s why we named it “Quality of Life...Insurance,” because it really can help improve the quality of life for our customers while they are living as well as the quality of their loved ones’ lives after they are gone.

Quality of Life Insurance

Our products at National Benefit Brokers are Changing the way Americans Think About, Purchase and Use Life Insurance.

Here are seven ways Quality of Life...Insurance can help improve your quality of life!

  1. Use your life insurance when you need it the most

    This is the most obvious and most unique feature of Quality of Life...Insurance. This policy can provide benefits to help pay the direct and indirect costs involved with covered critical, chronic and/or terminal illnesses or conditions.

  2. Offers Flexibility

    Quality of Life...Insurance can relieve the stress of returning to the workplace too soon, because it can pay in the event of a major heart attack, invasive cancer, stroke and other covered illnesses while the insured is still living.

  3. Generates Income

    Quality of Life...Insurance can help replace lost income for years to come, so that surviving family members can continue to pay for life's necessities.

  4. Creates Future Opportunities

    Quality of Life...Insurance can provide funding to start a business, or pay for schooling so surviving family members can train for a new career. It can also help fund long‐range goals such as college for grandchildren or a secure retirement.

  5. Provides a Fresh Start

    Quality of Life...Insurance allows loved ones to start with a clean financial slate by helping to pay off credit card bills, outstanding loans and even the mortgage.

  6. Buys Time

    After your death, Quality of Life...Insurance allows loved ones to focus on their grief by helping to pay for the funeral and other final expenses. After a covered critical illness, Quality of Life...Insurance can let you concentrate on what's most important ­ getting well.

  7. Leaves a Legacy

    Quality of Life...Insurance gives parents the chance to leave future generations with a true legacy of love ‐ long‐term financial security.
